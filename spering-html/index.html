Project 1

Requirement of this project

"Bhopal, known as 'The City of Lakes,' faces a grave environmental challenge due to the enduring impact of the Gas Tragedy. Surveys reveal persistent organic pollutants in water bodies, reaching 42 areas and posing health risks. MIC release during the tragedy continues to harm, with Swiss lab results showing alarming concentrations. The situation is worsening, affecting generations. Our study aims to assess the water quality in various lakes of Bhopal to address these pressing concerns."

How we approached it

"We initiated our study by analyzing key lakes in Bhopal, essential for public use. Each lake was assigned to a team for data collection using satellite imagery from Landstat and Sentinal via Google Earth Engine spanning from 2014 to 2023. Post data collection, a comprehensive analysis of various parameters provided insights into water quality post the Gas Tragedy. Our approach encompasses five main tasks: Planning and Analysis of Concerned Area, Data Acquisition, Data Analysis, and Dashboard Building and Validation."

What was the positive outcome

The comprehensive analysis of Bhopal's lakes, post Gas Tragedy, yielded valuable insights into water quality trends from 2014 to 2023. This study not only increased community awareness but also laid the groundwork for potential remediation efforts. Additionally, the creation of a monitoring dashboard enhances ongoing efforts to ensure the lakes' sustained environmental health.

Facts

Bhopal Gas Tragedy

In December 1984, the Bhopal Gas Tragedy unfolded at the Union Carbide pesticide plant, releasing lethal methyl isocyanate gas. The immediate impact led to thousands of casualties and long-term health issues for survivors, sparking global awareness about industrial safety and corporate responsibility.

Bhopal Gas Tragedy

The Bhopal Gas Tragedy, considered one of the worst industrial disasters, resulted from a toxic gas leak that occurred on the night of December 2-3, 1984. The aftermath saw widespread health issues, environmental contamination, and a lasting legacy of legal and ethical debates surrounding corporate accountability.

Bhopal Gas Tragedy

The legacy of the Bhopal Gas Tragedy endures, emphasizing the devastating consequences of industrial negligence. The incident, marked by inadequate safety measures and substandard maintenance.

Project 2

Requirement of this project

"The aim of the project is to use machine learning to study and analyze different types of road defects and to automatically detect any road abnormalities. We will design, build and test an inspection system for this purpose."

How we approached it

"The system is equipped with a camera to collect video streams from different roads with and without defects. Then, the captured data will be analyzed using the Matlab machine learning toolbox to train and test the network. Finally, the system will provide recommended actions for the municipality related to actions required to fix/correct road defects. The approach is divided into 4 main tasks: Data acquisition, Data Training/Testing, and Dashboard Building and Testing."

What was the positive outcome

Our advanced road inspection system, leveraging HD cameras and machine learning, automates defect detection, reducing time and effort. The system's data analysis provides actionable insights for efficient resource allocation, ensuring safer and better-maintained roads. The user-friendly dashboard facilitates real-time decision-making, contributing to improved transportation infrastructure and community well-being.

Resource-Intensive Practices

Current road inspection processes involve substantial time and labor. On average, manual inspections can take hours, often requiring a large workforce for comprehensive coverage.

Daily Deterioration Impact

With road surfaces degrading daily, statistics show a direct correlation between deteriorating infrastructure and economic losses. Regular inspections become paramount to address these issues promptly and mitigate economic impact.

Economic Efficiency at Stake

According to recent studies, inefficient road maintenance practices can contribute to a significant annual economic loss. Streamlining inspections with technology-driven solutions can result in substantial cost savings and enhance overall economic efficiency.
